My Story

I am from Ulundurpet, Tamil Nadu, and I come from a humble background where responsibilities became part of my life at an early age. I completed my 10th standard and finished my 12th standard in 2024 at a Government School, where I learned the value of discipline, hard work, and patience. After completing my 12th standard, I planned to continue my higher education; however, due to financial problems and family circumstances following my father's passing in 2019, I took responsibility to support my family instead of joining college. Even during these difficult times, I never stopped learning. My journey into technology started with zero coding knowledge, but after joining Freshworks STS Software Academy, I received hands-on training in full-stack web development. Through dedication, I developed myself as a Full-Stack Developer capable of building functional web applications. Today, my goal is to grow by contributing to meaningful projects and becoming a reliable member of a professional team. I believe responsibility, resilience, and continuous learning define who I am today.
